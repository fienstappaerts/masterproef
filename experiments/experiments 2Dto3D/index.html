    The step from 2D to 3D was very logical for me. I discovered this step last year in my bachelorproject where I took this relationship between both as my main subject. This year I wanted to include this step as well, similar to the sketches I went ahead and gotten experimental with SketchUp.

    From these videos I gotten with the idea of a new command that's in the system called 'extrude'. Starting from the idea of a 2D drawing and ending with a 3D structure with help from the extrude command that works on the y-axis.
